For M. C.
© 1998 Paulo Mouat
The auburn hair
With scent so sweet
It is yours my fair
That I love so deep
Your cherry lips
So gently kiss
it is their soft reaps
That mine do miss
Your almond eyes
Bring me afire
When cold as ice
My heart grows dire
Your marbled face
Of rosey white
Endows me grace
And great delight
With you my dove
Of great beauty be
I am in love
And that is my glee.
