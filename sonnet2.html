Sonnet II
© 1998 Paulo Mouat
O today I am indeed at a loss,
Yet of yearning I feel my heart replete.
Bereft of love, my sorrow grieves and woes
As without you I am deemed incomplete.
Yesterday we met, of two hearts made one,
A fleeting bliss now sadness turned forlorn;
An elusion memory will have won,
And recall, how all is fickle and shorn.
And what future for this despairing plea,
Honing for the little that does remain?
Tomorrow is left for hope; hope that we,
by heavens past, should bond together again.
You are my joy, my happiness my all,
In you, my beloved, my heart does fall.
